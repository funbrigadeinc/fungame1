Description

Each Child hood pic of an associate was matched to an alphabet and the same way all the associate pictures were assigned to alphabets. Match the child hood pic to its respective associate to get a combination of two characters. Ex: childhood pic of an associate mapped to 'A'. Respective associate pic was mapped to 'T'. For this combination you will get two characters "AT" or "TA". This "AT" or "TA" would be part of the challenge answer. This way match all the childhood pics with the respective associates to get the final answer.
